Getting Started
Obtain a Money Press - Purchase or craft a money press item
Gather Required Materials - You'll need paper, ink, and some cash to operate
Find a Safe Location - Choose where to set up your illegal operation
Start Printing - Begin the 10-minute printing process
Collect Your Money - Receive $150-250 in counterfeit bills

To operate the money press, you need:

Paper x5 - Blank sheets for printing
Ink x2 - Printing ink for the bills

Using The Money Press
Approach your placed money press
Target it and select "Use Money Press"
Choose "Start Printing Money" (requires materials)
Wait 10 minutes for the process to complete
Packing Up
Target your money press
Select "Pack Up Money Press"
Wait for the pack-up animation
The press returns to your inventory

Police Alerts - There's a % chance police will be notified of suspicious activity
One Press Only - You can only have one money press placed at a time
